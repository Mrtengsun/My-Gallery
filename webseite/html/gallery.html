"OrientalRock" is a series of oriental styles that are cool and rock.
The series showcases oriental characters, oriental realness and oriental fashion with a historical point of view. This collection is also an expression of oriental culture, self-confidence and self-awareness. It represents a world where domination politics, eurocentrism and racism do not exist. A world for all cultures, for acceptence, equality and diversity.
"OrientalRock" was born in it's digital form, where I combined something new with something old. I introduced multiperiod styles to a contemporary digital world dominated by pixels. Like with history you can only fully observe the pieces of the collection from a distance, but are unable to see them clearly up close. They are the horizon, not the present.
"OrientalRock" plays with stereotypes and representations of oriental characters from all eras, ethnicities, professions, religions, fashions, social status and genders. They can be facsinating to everyone and every culture.
